Bluesky, however, wants to focus more on the tools it provides users to control their own experience. Today, this includes things like moderation lists that let users quickly block a group of people they don’t want to interact with, content filter controls, muted words, and the ability to subscribe to other moderation service providers. Canva said that it is launching its own foundational model, trained on its elements, that would generate designs with editable layers and objects as compared to a flat image. It said the model works across different formats, including social media posts, presentations, whiteboards, and websites. “We started by creating flat images with diffusion models. The platform is now making that assistant available across screens, including the design and elements tabs.
