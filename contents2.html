LEGO SMART Bricks introduce a new way to build — and they don’t require screens

요약

LEGO has unveiled its new SMART Play system at CES, introducing interactive and responsive LEGOs that do not require screens. The system features a 2x4 SMART Brick, SMART Tag tiles, and SMART Minifigures. These components can sense nearby SMART Tags, which have unique digital IDs that dictate the Bricks' and Minifigures' actions. For instance, a SMART Brick in a helicopter set will light up and make propeller sounds, with an accelerometer ensuring sounds and lights align with play movements. The SMART Bricks are powered by a patented ASIC chip, utilizing near-field magnetic positioning, a miniature speaker, accelerometer, and LED array. LEGO also developed BrickNet, a Bluetooth-based protocol for multiple SMART Bricks to interact, featuring enhanced encryption and privacy controls. The system is designed for easy setup, with no screens involved, and its first two Star Wars-themed sets are set to launch on March 1st, with preorders opening soon.

TII’s Falcon H1R 7B can out-reason models up to 7x its size — and it’s (mostly) open

요약

The Technology Innovation Institute (TII) in Abu Dhabi has released Falcon H1R 7B, a 7-billion parameter AI model that challenges the conventional belief that larger models are necessary for better reasoning. Falcon H1R 7B utilizes a hybrid architecture, integrating the Mamba state-space model (SSM) architecture with standard Transformer attention layers, allowing it to outperform models nearly seven times its size, such as Alibaba's Qwen and Nvidia's Nemotron. This release signifies a shift in the open-weight AI ecosystem towards architectural efficiency and inference-time scaling rather than just raw parameter count. The model's code is available on Hugging Face, and a live demo is accessible via Falcon Chat. TII has also published a comprehensive technical report detailing its approach and training methodology.

요약

Google TV is receiving a significant Gemini update that introduces AI-powered image and video generation, along with voice control for settings, initially rolling out to TCL smart TVs. The Google TV platform will connect with Google Photos, allowing Gemini to access and remix images, generate slideshows, or transform still photos into videos using voice commands. While offering a fun distraction, Google acknowledges that this might not be a core TV experience, but the quality of its AI models could provide benefits on a larger screen.

Hackers claim breach of engineering firm, offer sale of info on three major US utilities

요약

Hackers are claiming a breach of Pickett and Associates, a Florida-based civil engineering firm, and are offering to sell over 800 sensitive engineering files allegedly stolen from the company. These files are reportedly tied to major U.S. utilities and include LiDAR point clouds, orthophotos, design files, and transmission corridor maps, with a price tag of approximately $600,000. Duke Energy is currently investigating the claims. The attackers are also selling data from Germany’s Enerparc AG, indicating a focus on critical infrastructure. Pickett and Associates provides services like transmission and distribution design, surveying, and aerial mapping to various utilities across the U.S. and the Caribbean.
