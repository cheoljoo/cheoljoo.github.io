D&B's database of 642 million businesses was built for humans, not AI agents. So they rebuilt it.

요약

Dun & Bradstreet (D&B) rebuilt its Commercial Graph database, which covers 642 million businesses, because its original design for human analysts was unsuitable for AI agents. The previous architecture, a collection of fragmented systems with custom integrations, could not handle the sub-second latency and direct querying required by AI agents in workflows like credit and supply chain management. The database's rapid growth to over 642 million records with 11,000 fields per record further complicated the issue for AI.
